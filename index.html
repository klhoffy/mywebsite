Case Studies

SomatiColors

An app made to help people with Somatoform to track symptoms

New

SomatiColors is an app to help people with Somatoform, a disorder that is a mental illness that causes bodily symptoms, including pain. The symptoms can't be traced back to any physical cause. This app lets the user connect what they're feeling when a particular pain occurs.

SomatiColors was a true collaboration between me and two amazing developers, Jessica and Miwa. Although we did small pieces on our own, about 80% of the programming was done together on one computer. My biggest single contribution in the project was full CRUD on the Events in AngularJS.

Show Me Your Junk App

Every week in Los Angeles a fortune of money and energy is spent moving 12,000 tons of waste to Puente Hills landfill, the largest rubbish dump in America. This app allows people to post their free stuff on our site and others can find these products and can respond through Facebook comments and see where to go with Google Maps.

Catherine Lenore UX Design

A needlepoint / needle work website that incorporates the option to buy kits and canvasses. It will also have a blog about new design ideas and have a revolving roster of artists. Most of the products will be made from these artists and put onto canvases. At some point it will let the consumer create their own image to add to a canvas. Most of the needlepoint websites around are tragic to look at and would discourage any young person interested from wanting to buy needlepoint. I want a site that encourages the youngsters.

Smurf-O-Vision 2 App UX Design

I created this wireframe for the Blu-ray release of the Smurfs 2 movie as an app that can be used while watching the disc. Half of the app is a game that syncs with wifi or audio to the disc when it plays and lets the user play a game with the movie. The other half of the app lets the user make their own mini Smurfs movie with characters, sound effects and special effects.
